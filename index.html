Our Story

Crafted with Purpose, Worn with Pride

Velvets & Threads was born from a deep love for India's rich textile heritage. Founded in 2019 by sisters Priya and Ananya Sharma, our boutique brings together master craftspeople from across India—from the looms of Banaras to the embroidery workshops of Lucknow.

Every piece in our collection is handpicked, ensuring the highest quality of craftsmanship while providing sustainable livelihoods to artisan communities. We believe in slow fashion, in pieces that tell stories and become heirlooms.
